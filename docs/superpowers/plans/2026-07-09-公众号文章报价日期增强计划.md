# 公众号文章报价日期增强 Implementation Plan

> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development (recommended) or superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan task-by-task. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax for tracking.

**Goal:** Add `quote_date` so article publish time, quote business date, and collection time are handled separately.

**Architecture:** Keep `collect_time` from raw collection, parse real WeChat `publish_time` from page metadata or visible `#publish_time`, and derive `quote_date` during analysis. Persist the three time concepts on article analysis and egg price detail rows, then make article daily report tasks and stats use `quote_date` with publish-date fallback.

**Tech Stack:** Python 3.12, Playwright, SQLAlchemy, PyMySQL, MySQL 8, pytest.

## Global Constraints

- Do not change the first-version OCR boundary; no OCR dependency is added.
- Do not touch `wechat_group_*` tables or group pipeline code.
- Do not output article body, HTML, links, screenshots, OCR raw text, or full quote lists in CLI/log/report output.
- Use `quote_date` as the poultry-egg quote business date; keep `publish_time/publish_date` for WeChat source traceability and `collect_time` for collection audit.
